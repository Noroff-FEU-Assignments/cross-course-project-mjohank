About us

Who are we?

Rainydays is a collective of Norwegian designers and adventurers. brought together by a common desire: to bring Norwegian design and quality to the masses. We firmly believe that quality can come at a reasonable price.

Sustainability

Environmental sustainability is a vital part of our business model. We pride ourselves on our low environmental footprint. At the same time, it is important to maintain a continuous focus on sustainability, which is why we have set a goal to be the first jacket company with zero carbon emission within 2028.
